What We Do

Data Center Services

Data Center Services help enterprises provision, manage, and address the organizational needs to ensure continuous delivery of data center operations and management services to their customers and end users for all mission- and business-critical applications. Our domain expertise and dedicated Centers of Excellence help you consolidate, virtualize, transform, optimize, and compute network storage and backup capabilities.

IT Operations Management

IT organizations are under tremendous pressure today to use existing resources optimally. The challenge is to proactively perform infrastructure and application monitoring and root-cause analysis to identify underlying cause and provide permanent solution. Proactive industry standard application and infrastructure monitoring, and detailed root-cause analysis help reduce the repair time and cost of IT operations.
IT Operations Management services integrate your IT infrastructure with the run side of the business, achieving efficiency in running the business independent of the people supporting it. Business-critical services are monitored and prioritized through a domain oriented approach. Our compliance to the ITIL framework and dedicated support for packaged software and fragmented applications, along with experience in delivering collaborative operations are key enablers.
